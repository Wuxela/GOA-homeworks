My Favorite Movies

Need for Speed (2014)

Description: An action-packed movie based on street racing, following Tobey Marshall's journey of revenge.

Why I like it: It has awesome cars, speed, great music, and intense storylines. Most of all — you can feel the adrenaline and the love for racing cars.

Watch on IMDB

Gran Turismo (2023)

Description: A true story of a gamer who becomes a real-life professional racer through the Gran Turismo video game.

Why I like it: It’s motivational and exciting. It shows how a dream can come true through passion and dedication. Plus, the racing scenes are epic!

Watch on IMDB

Ford v Ferrari (2019)

Description: Based on a true story, this film shows how Ford challenged Ferrari at the 24 Hours of Le Mans race.

Why I like it: It's full of emotion, history, and incredible racing. The characters — especially Carroll Shelby and Ken Miles — are inspiring, and the racing scenes are thrilling.
