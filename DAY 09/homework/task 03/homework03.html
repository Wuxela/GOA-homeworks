The Resident Evil 7: Biohazard

  • Release Year: 2017
  • Platforms: PS4, Xbox One, PC, Nintendo Switch (Cloud), PS5/Xbox Series (via backward compatibility)
  • Genre: Survival Horror
  • Perspective: First-person
  • Developer: Capcom
  • Engine: RE Engine (debuted with this game)

Plot Overview (Spoiler-Free)

  • You play as Ethan Winters,
  • an ordinary man who receives a mysterious message from his wife Mia, who was thought to be dead for the past three years.
  • The message leads him to a run-down plantation in Dulvey,
  • Louisiana, where he discovers the Baker family, a group of deranged and mutated people. As Ethan searches for Mia,
  • he gets caught in a nightmare of bio-organic horror, hidden labs, and unkillable enemies.

Main Characters

  • Ethan Winters
  • The protagonist. An average man thrown into a terrifying situation.
  • Ethan Winters
  • Ethan's missing wife. Something is very wrong with her.
  • Jack Baker
  • The terrifying, nearly invincible patriarch.
  • Marguerite Baker
  • Controls insects. Hides dark secrets.
  • Lucas Baker
  • Sadistic and mentally unstable. Loves “games.”
  • Eveline
  • A mysterious little girl with enormous power.

What Makes It Unique

  • First-person perspective – a major shift from previous Resident Evil games.
  • Back to survival horror – slow-paced, scary, and full of tension.
  • Classic RE elements – puzzles, inventory management, healing items, save rooms.
  • Biohazard horror – connected to viruses and bioweapons, not supernatural.
  • VR support (PSVR) – full game can be played in virtual reality.

Gameplay Features

  • Combat: Limited weapons and ammo – every bullet counts.
  • Exploration: Find keys, hidden paths, and solve puzzles.
  • Enemies: The Baker family and twisted monsters called the “Molded.”
  • Crafting: Combine items to make healing kits or ammo.
  • Resource Management: Small inventory space forces careful choices.

Main Locations

  • Guest House – Where it all begins (and gets insane quickly).
  • Main House – The Baker family home, full of puzzles and traps.
  • Old House – A bug-infested nightmare.
  • Testing Area – A deadly funhouse of traps from Lucas.
  • Shipwreck and Lab – Reveals the true origin of Eveline and the virus.
