The Resident Evil 2 Remake

  • Release Date: January 25, 2019
  • Platforms: PS4, Xbox One, PC, PS5, Xbox Series (Enhanced)
  • Engine: RE Engine
  • Genre: Survival Horror
  • Perspective: Over-the-shoulder third-person (like RE4)
  • Developer/Publisher: Capcom

Story Summary (Spoiler-Free)

  • et in Raccoon City, the game follows two characters:
  • Leon S. Kennedy – A rookie cop reporting for his first day.
  • Claire Redfield – A college student searching for her missing brother, Chris (from RE1).

  • Both arrive in the city during a zombie outbreak caused by a leak of the T-Virus, a biological weapon created by the Umbrella Corporation.
  • They become trapped in the city and must survive through puzzles, monsters, and uncover the truth behind the outbreak — all while being hunted by Tyrant (Mr. X).

Two Campaigns

  • 🧔 Leon’s Campaign: More military/weapon-focused story. Interacts with Ada Wong.
  • 👩 Claire’s Campaign: More emotionally driven. Helps Sherry Birkin, a young girl.
  • ou can play both characters’ stories in “A” and “B” scenarios, offering different perspectives, item placements, and boss encounters.

Gameplay Highlights

  • 🎮 Survival Horror at its finest:
  • Limited ammo & healing items
  • Inventory management
  • Typewriter saves (classic style)
  • 🎥 Over-the-shoulder view (like Resident Evil 4) — modern yet terrifying
  • 🧠 Environmental puzzles:
  • Find medallions
  • Restore power
  • Unlock secret paths
  • 🔫 Upgradable weapons:
  • Custom parts (laser sights, extended mags, etc.)
  • Leon gets handguns and shotguns
  • Claire gets revolvers and grenade launcher

Main Locations

  • Raccoon City Police Department (RPD) – iconic, labyrinthine building
  • Underground Labs – ties into Umbrella’s bioweapon experiments
  • Sewers – filled with dangerous creatures
  • Orphanage (Claire) – creepy, story-driven area
