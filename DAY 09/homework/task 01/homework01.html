The Resident Evil Village

Resident Evil Village (also known as Resident Evil 8) is a survival horror video game developed and published by Capcom.
It was released on May 7, 2021, for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, and PC.

Quick Overview:

  • Genre: Survival Horror
  • Perspective: First-person
  • Main Character: Ethan Winters
  • Sequel To: Resident Evil 7: Biohazard

Story Summary:

  • After surviving the events of Resident Evil 7,
  • Ethan Winters is living peacefully with his wife, Mia.
  • However, their lives are shattered when Chris Redfield unexpectedly appears and kidnaps their baby daughter, Rosemary.
  • Ethan finds himself in a mysterious Eastern European village filled with nightmarish creatures and must fight to rescue his daughter.

Main Enemies & Lords:

  • The village is ruled by four lords, each with their own twisted domain:
  • Lady Dimitrescu – A tall, vampiric noblewoman in a gothic castle.
  • Donna Beneviento – Controls creepy dolls in a haunted house.
  • Salvatore Moreau – A mutated fish-like monster.
  • Karl Heisenberg – A mad scientist with magnetic powers.
  • They all serve the mysterious cult leader Mother Miranda, who has plans for Ethan's daughter.

Gameplay Features:

  • Exploration and puzzle-solving.
  • Intense combat with limited resources.
  • A merchant called The Duke who helps upgrade weapons and craft supplies.
  • A mix of gothic horror, action, and mystery.
